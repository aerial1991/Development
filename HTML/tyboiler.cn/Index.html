  • 新一代能循环流化床锅炉的四大技术优势： Four Major Technical Advantages of the New Generation CFBed Boiler

    Diverse fuel sources: Successfully achieved the use of "zero-carbon" and "low-carbon" fuels in circulating fluidized bed boilers.

    In-furnace ultra-low emissions: Actively achieving wide-load, low-cost ultra-low emissions of SO2 and NOx during the combustion process.

    Ultra-high efficiency: Capturing industry pain points, independently developing core auxiliary equipment, and improving efficiency.

    Flexibility and intelligence: Overcoming key bottleneck issues such as ultra-low load rates and high load variation rates in circulating fluidized bed boilers.

  • 太锅集团发展理念 Development Concept of Taiyuan Boiler Group

    Taiyuan Boiler Group adheres to the "full production factors, full life cycle, full industry chain, full value chain" to create a low-carbon thermal power and energy supply full industry chain business model integrating "core technology research and development, advanced equipment manufacturing, engineering total package operation and maintenance, investment operation and management".

  • 核心技术发展战略 Core Technology Development Strategy

    Breakthroughs have been achieved in three core technologies of circulating fluidized bed boilers: "intelligent control, PC-CFB combustion, and subcritical steam parameters". Taiyuan Boiler Group continues to maintain the world's leading level in the performance of coal-fired circulating fluidized bed boiler units.
